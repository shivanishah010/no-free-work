Resources for Freelancers

Here are some useful resources where freelancers can find tools, conversations, and communities around all things freelancing rates.

Rate Calculator

Creator Pricing Calculator

This incredibly handy calculator helps creatives and freelancers calculate sustainable rates based on monthly expenses, taxes, and billable time.


You can use it to work out rates per hour, day, month, or project.

Built by: Stella Achenbach

Research Reports

New

The 2025 Freelancer Rates Report

Here's a rare research report that covers a wide range of freelance professions and industries. Alongside the usually represented crew of content writers, designers, and developers you have data for sound engineers, data scientists, events managers, storyboard artists, CRM consultants...


Get information about day rates, experience levels, contract lengths and bookings, and highest paid skills.

Built by: The team at YunoJuno

Strategic Services

New

Clarity Action Plan

Pricing confidence starts with clarity on your positioning. The Clarity Action Plan from Tasmin Lofthouse is a 90-minute strategy session to help you align your brand positioning with your goals, services, and audience.


Get the clarity you need to be more confident about your rates and have better, more profitable conversations with prospects.

Offered by: Tasmin Lofthouse
